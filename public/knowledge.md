## About Us

Reach Healthcare Solutions is a specialist domiciliary care provider working with individuals and families to help them reach their potential — maintaining dignity and improving quality of life.

We have over 40 years of combined experience as registered nurses caring for children and adults with various disabilities and complex care needs.

We were born out of frustration with fractured services for families with children with complex healthcare needs. We are based in London and provide services across the whole of the UK.

## Care Services

### Children & Young People
We provide practical, specialist care for children and young people with complex healthcare, emotional, and behavioural needs. Our team has hands-on experience dealing with challenging behaviours and delivering high-quality, compassionate support.

### Domiciliary Care
Professional support with daily activities in the comfort of your own home. This includes personal care, medication management, shopping, meal preparation, and companionship — helping individuals maintain independence.

### Live-In Care
A full-time carer lives in your home to provide round-the-clock support. This is a popular and cost-effective alternative to residential care, particularly for couples who wish to stay together.

### Hospital to Home
We support a smooth and safe transition from hospital back to home, ensuring continuity of care, reducing the risk of readmission, and helping individuals regain confidence and independence.

### Respite Care
Short-term care that gives family caregivers a well-deserved break, with the peace of mind that their loved one is in safe, professional hands.

### End of Life Care
Compassionate, dignified end-of-life care for individuals and their families. We focus on comfort, respect, and emotional support during this deeply personal time.

### Supported Living
We empower individuals with disabilities or complex needs to live independently with the right level of tailored support — enabling a life full of purpose and meaning.

### Specialist Care
Expert care for adults with complex health conditions, physical disabilities, and specialised medical needs. Our staff are trained in a wide range of clinical specialties.
